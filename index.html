The Story

Joey and Zach met at UCSD because there were both part of the same student organization, Alpha Phi Omega. From there, the two found a shared passion for game design and started to work on a prototype for a simple mobile game. After taking time to study for finals and abandoning the project temporarily, the group reconvened, formed Seven Pixel Studios DBA, and Battle Paddles was born.
